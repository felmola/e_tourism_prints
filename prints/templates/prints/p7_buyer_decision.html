----> Conditionally on: Treatment "Control" and "Commercial practice" <----

Once you click on the "Buy" button your payment will be calculated for this trading round.

----> Conditionally on: Treatment "Informal Sanction" <----

Remember that you have the ability to report the untruthful use of commercial practices by a seller. If you believe that a seller has used the commercial practices untruthfully, please check that seller on the next page.

If at least 3 buyers report the same seller for using a commercial practice untruthfully, the seller will have to pay a fine. This fine will be 0.6 ECU plus 0.2 ECU for each additional buyer who reports it. The amount of the fine will be divided equally among the buyers who reported the seller.

Once you click on the "Buy" button, you will be see the next page where you can report a seller if you wish.

----> Conditionally on: "Formal" Treatment <----

We will audit two of the sellers randomly, i.e. the probability of being audited is 20%. If an audited seller is using some commercial practice untruthfully, the seller must pay 2 ECUs as a fine. These 2 ECUs will be distributed among the buyers. Once you click on the "Buy" button, we the investigators will go through the process of checking to see if any buyer has untruthfully used any commercial practices. We will then calculate your payment for this round of negotiation.
